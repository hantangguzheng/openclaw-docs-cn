📝 提示词编写指南

从入门到精通，掌握 OpenClaw 提示词的核心技巧

什么是 Prompt？

Prompt（提示词）是与 AI 沟通的指令。高质量的 Prompt 可以让 AI 准确理解你的需求，生成你想要的结果。

基础语法

Generate a video of: [主体描述] , [环境/场景] , [风格] , [镜头运动] , [质量修饰]

💡 黄金法则

用英文写 Prompt！虽然 OpenClaw 支持中文理解，但 AI 模型在英文语境下训练更充分，英文 Prompt 效果更好。

主体描述（Subject）

描述你想要的主要对象。使用具体、细节的词汇。

✅ 好的示例

a professional basketball player performing a powerful slam dunk, jumping high above the rim, white jersey number 23, muscles tensed, intense focused expression, dynamic pose mid-air

❌ 差的示例

a person playing basketball

环境与场景（Environment）

描述主体所处的环境、光线和氛围。

# 环境描述示例 modern indoor basketball arena spotlight dramatic lighting from above crowd cheering loudly in the background confetti falling from the ceiling clean polished hardwood floor

镜头运动（Camera Movement）

# 镜头运动关键词 dynamic camera movement, tracking shot → 动感追踪 slow motion, 240fps, time freeze → 慢动作 aerial drone shot, bird's eye view → 航拍 extreme close-up, macro detail → 特写 wide establishing shot, panoramic → 全景 dolly tracking shot → 跟拍

质量修饰词（Quality Modifiers）

添加这些词可以提升生成质量：

8K, hyperrealistic, studio lighting volumetric lighting, ray tracing RAW photo, Canon EOS R5 Fujifilm color science color grading, film grain award winning photography

完整示例

Generate a video of a professional basketball player performing a powerful slam dunk, leaping high above the rim, white jersey number 23, muscles tensed, intense focused expression, dynamic pose mid-air, in a modern indoor basketball arena, spotlight dramatic lighting from above, crowd cheering loudly in the background, cinematic, film still, anamorphic lens flare, dynamic camera movement, tracking shot following the player, 8K, hyperrealistic, studio lighting, color grading

进阶技巧

🔢 使用数字量化

用数字描述动作速度和效果："player running at full sprint speed" 比 "player running fast" 更有效。

🎨 善用参考

提到你喜欢的电影或艺术家风格："in the style of Christopher Nolan's cinematography" 可以帮助 AI 理解你想要的效果。

⏱️ 控制时长

对于视频，指定时长有助于 AI 合理分配内容节奏："first 3 seconds: intro, next 5 seconds: action, last 2 seconds: climax"。

🔗 组合多个元素

不要只描述一个主体，可以加入多个元素互动："a basketball player dodging a defender while another teammate sets a screen"。
